劉吳月華姊妹生平

母親劉吳月華姊妹生於一九二八年八月十八日，祖籍中國廣東省梅縣。曾就讀於梅縣師範中學，一九四九年與劉富華先生結婚，兩人隨即離鄉到香港建立家庭。 

母親與丈夫經營畫廊生意，生活艱苦，在逆境中與神相遇。憑著信心，克苦耐勞丶勤儉樂觀，面對任何生活挑戰。

母親育有三男三女。她常自豪說 : 「女丶仔丶女丶仔丶女丶仔」梅花間竹；感謝 神奇妙的安排! 神祝福她享受四代同堂之福! 現有六名孫兒女及三名曾孫 。

一九九六年與父親退休移民到加拿大，協助子女生活上的需要，弄孫為樂 。二零零八年，母親因跌倒需以輪椅代步，遷進安老院居住。母親性格開朗，富同情心丶和藹可親。 每天自己滾動輪椅，逐一房間去探望院友，甚至幫忙餵食丶拿暖開水給他們。她樂於助人，熱心幫忙 ; 每天零晨起床摺毛巾丶打理餐桌丶淋花澆水。差不多安老院所有員工都認識媽媽 Ng。她笑口常開，善於與人交談。 其中一員工說: 「媽媽 Ng 富有生命力，她的笑容從沒間斷 ! 」母親常常感恩，不忘傳福音，分享自己生命與神的經歷。常問人:「信主未呀？快啲去聽書啦，很好的呀！」

今年三月中，在疫情期間，女兒將母親接回家與孫兒一起居往。大家都滿懷喜樂，溫暖和諧，幾乎天天可與其他在海外的家人視像通話唱詩，留下很多美好回憶。每當看見曾孫兒的視像時，母親都興奮雀躍，返老還童。母親常掛幸福笑臉，充滿恩典。

八月中母親因吸入性肺炎住院醫治，勇敢面對病魔，倚靠神的扶助，面對神的主權說:「一切有主的擔當! 」二零二零年十月二十三日，在家人陪伴下，母親安祥地被天父接回天家,享年九十三歲。


A blessed life of Mrs. Yit-Wah NG LAU

Our mother, Mrs. Yit-Wah NG LAU , was born on August 18, 1928. Her hometown was Meixian, Guangdong of mainland China. She studied at  Meizhou Women’s Normal School.  In 1949, she met Mr. Fu-Wah LAU, our father. They got married and left their hometown shortly after the wedding. They moved to Hong Kong and established their own family.

Our parents worked very hard. Together they ran a gallery business - dad was the artist and mother was the salesperson. In those years they experienced much hardships. In the midst of their adversity, they had a personal encounter with God. Since then our mother faced life challenges with her confidence, hardworking, virtue of frugalness and optimism.

Our mother gave birth to three sons and three daughters. She was often proud to say, "daughter, son, daughter, son, daughter, son - “plum-blossom-bamboo (梅花間竹; alternatively)！Thank God for the wonderful arrangement! “  God also blessed her to enjoy the blessing of four generations - six grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.

In 1996, our mother and father retired. They moved to Canada to help their children with family matters. In 2008, our mother had to move to a senior home because she fell and had to use a wheelchair.  Our mother remained cheerful and compassionate. She was strong enough to roll her wheelchair everywhere, room by room she visited the residents.  She was willing to help others enthusiastically, and she even helped feeding them! Our mother kept her hardworking spirit.  She got up early every morning and volunteered to fold towels, to clean up table and water the plants. Almost all the employees of the nursing home know our mother.  She often laughed and socialized with people.  One of the employees said, "Mama Ng is full of vitality, and her smile never stops! “  Our mother always showed her gratefulness to the Lord for her salvation. She constantly shared the gospel, her life stories or her experience in God.  She often asked people with whom she encountered , "Have you trusted Jesus as your personal savior?  Go to church and listen to the bible. It's good for you."

In mid-March this year when the pandemic started to get worse, we decided to bring our mother home to stay with her family. Everyone was full of joy, warmth and harmony. She could often connect with other overseas family members through video conferencing. In those days we all had a lot of good memories. Whenever our mother saw her great-grandchildren through video conferencing calls, she got very excited and showed her happiness like a child.

In mid-August, our mother was hospitalized due to aspiration pneumonia. She faced the illness bravely. She relied on God and submitted to God's sovereignty and said,”God bears all our burdens!"  On October 23rd, 2020 accompanied by family, our mother went to heavenly home peacefully at the age of 93.
