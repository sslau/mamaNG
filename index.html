劉吳月華女士生平

母親劉吳月華女士生於一九二八年八月十八日，祖籍中國廣東省梅縣。曾就讀於梅縣師範中學，一九四九年與劉富華先生結婚，兩人隨即離鄉到香港建立家庭。 

母親與丈夫經營畫廊生意，生活艱苦，在逆境中與神相遇。憑著信心，克苦耐勞丶勤儉樂觀，面對任何生活挑戰。

母親育有三男三女。她常自豪說 :＂女丶仔丶女丶仔丶女丶仔，梅花間竹；感謝 神奇妙的安排! 神祝福她享受四代同堂之福! 現有六名孫兒女及三名曾孫 。

一九九六年與父親退休移民到加拿大，協助子女生活上的需要，弄孫為樂 。二零零八年，母親因跌倒需以輪椅代步，遷進安老院居住。母親性格開朗，富同情心丶和藹可親。 每天自己滾動輪椅，逐一房間去探望院友，甚至幫忙餵食丶拿暖開水給他們。她樂於助人，熱心幫忙 ; 每天零晨起床摺毛巾丶打理餐桌丶淋花澆水。差不多安老院所有員工都認識媽媽 Ng。她笑口常開，善於與人交談。 其中一員工說: " 媽媽 Ng 富有生命力，她的笑容從沒間斷 ! ” 母親常常感恩，不忘傳福音，分享自己生命與神的經歷。常問人:"信主未呀？快啲去聽書啦，很好的呀！”

今年三月中，在疫情期間，女兒將母親接回家與孫兒一起居往。大家都滿懷喜樂，溫暖和諧，幾乎天天可與其他在海外的家人視像通話唱詩，留下很多美好回憶。每當看見曾孫兒的視像時，母親都興奮雀躍，返老還童。母親常掛幸福笑臉，充滿恩典。

八月中母親因吸入性肺炎住院醫治，勇敢面對病魔，倚靠神的扶助，面對神的主權說 "一切有主的擔當!”二零二零年十月二十三日，在家人陪伴下，母親安祥地被天父接回天家,享年九十三歲。


Mrs. Yit Wah Ng

Our mother, Mrs. Yit-Wah NG LAU , was born on August 18, 1928. Her hometown was Muiyuen, Guangdong of mainland China. She studied at Muiyuen Teacher training School. In 1949, she met Mr. Fu-Wah LAU, our father.  They got married and left their hometown shortly after the wedding.  Then moved to Hong Kong and established their own family.

Our parents worked very hard.  Together they ran a gallery business - dad was the artist and mother was the salesperson.  In those years they experienced much hardships.  In the midst of their adversity, they had a personal encounter with God.  Since then mother faced life challenges with confidence, hardworking, frugalness , and optimism.

Mother has three sons and three daughters.  She was often proud to say, "daughter, son, daughter, son, daughter, son - plum blossom bamboo; thank you for the wonderful arrangement! “ God blessed her to enjoy the blessing of four generations! She had six grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.

Retired to Canada with our father in 1996 to help their children living.  In 2008, his mother had to move to senior home because she fell and had to sit on a wheelchair.  My mother is cheerful and compassionate.  She strong enough to roll the wheelchairs every day, visit the residents room by room, and even help feeding them.  She is willing to help others enthusiastically; she gets up every morning, folds the towels, clean up table and watering the plants.  Almost all the employees in the nursing home know mama Ng. She often laughs and socialize with people.  One of them said, "Mama Ng is full of vitality, and her smile never stops!  Our mother is often grateful, never forgets to preach the gospel, and shares her life and God's experience. She often asks other, " Do you believe in God?  Go and listen to the bible.  It's good for you."

In mid-March this year, during the pandemic, mom was bought home to stay with her family.  Everyone is full of joy, warmth and harmony, and also connected with other overseas family members often, leaving a lot of good memories.  Whenever mom saw her great-grandchildren through conference call, mom was excited.

In mid-August, my mother was hospitalized for aspiration pneumonia, she faced the disease bravely, relied on God, and submitted to God's sovereignty, "God bears all our burdens!"  On October 23rd, 2020, accompanied by family, our mother went to heavenly home at the age of 93.
